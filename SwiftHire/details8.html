Sales & Business Development

SwiftHire Partners • Molyko, Buea

About the Role

We aren’t looking for a "pushy salesperson" who just wants to close a deal. We need a relationship builder who understands that business in Buea is built on trust and a good handshake. You’ll be our voice in the community, connecting local businesses with solutions that actually help them grow. If you love meeting new people and turning a "maybe" into a "yes," this is the spot for you.

What you'll be doing

  • Heading out into the Molyko and Mile 17 hubs to meet with business owners and understand the challenges they face.
  • Representing our brand at local tech meetups and community events, making sure everyone knows how we can help them succeed.
  • Following up with our partners to ensure they’re getting real results and building the kind of long-term trust that defines our brand.

Requirements

You’re a natural talker, but more importantly, you’re an excellent listener. You should be the kind of person who is resilient, positive, and doesn’t take "no" personally—you see it as a chance to try a different conversation. If you have a deep understanding of what makes Buea businesses tick and you’re hungry to see our ecosystem scale, let's talk.
