Content Writer (Tech & Business)

Silicon Mountain News • Remote / Molyko Office

About the Role

Silicon Mountain is full of incredible stories, but most of them haven’t been told yet. We aren’t looking for a "corporate reporter" who writes dry, boring articles. We need a storyteller who can dive deep into our local tech scene and write things that people actually want to read. You’ll be the voice of the ecosystem, sharing the wins and the lessons of our founders and builders.

What you'll be doing

  • Sitting down with local developers and startup founders to uncover the "why" behind their projects.
  • Writing weekly blog posts, newsletters, and reports that highlight the growth of business in the South West.
  • Keeping our brand voice consistent and sharp—making sure everything we publish is clear, professional, and full of life.

Requirements

You love to write, and it shows. Whether it’s your personal blog, a collection of LinkedIn posts, or a private journal, you should have a way with words. You should be the kind of person who stays curious about what’s happening in Molyko and enjoys breaking down complex business ideas into simple, engaging stories. If you have a sharp eye for detail and a heart for local tech, we want you on our team.
