Registered Nurse (General Practice)

Molyko Community Clinic • Molyko, Buea

About the Role

Healthcare in Buea is about more than just medicine; it’s about empathy and community. We are looking for someone who doesn't just see a patient file, but sees a neighbor. You’ll be on the front lines, providing care in a busy environment where your kindness matters just as much as your clinical skill. If you're a new graduate looking for a place to truly start your journey, we want to meet you.

What you'll be doing

  • Providing hands-on care and comfort to patients, ensuring they feel safe and heard during their visit.
  • Administering treatments and keeping accurate, organized records to help our small team run smoothly.
  • Educating families in our community on healthy habits and preventive care so we can grow a healthier Buea together.

Requirements

You must be certified and licensed to practice nursing. Beyond the certificate, we are looking for someone with a patient heart and the ability to stay calm when the clinic gets busy. If you treat every patient like they are your own family member, you’ll fit right in with our team.
