Social Media & Growth Lead

Zest Digital Agency • Check-point, Buea

About the Role

We don’t need a "content robot" to just post pictures and hope for likes. We need a community builder. You are the bridge between our brand and the people of Buea. Your job is to find the right voice, the right memes, and the right timing to make us the talk of Molyko and beyond. If you’re the person who always knows what’s trending before everyone else, we want you on our team.

What you'll be doing

  • Running our content calendar across WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram, making sure we stay consistent and relevant.
  • Creating short, catchy videos and graphics that explain what we do in a way that feels human and relatable to the local audience.
  • Replying to comments and messages to ensure every follower feels heard and part of our growing community.

Requirements

You are a digital native. You know exactly what makes people in Cameroon click, share, and comment. You should have excellent communication skills in English (and a little bit of Pidgin for that local flavor when it's needed). Most importantly, you should be a creative thinker who can turn a boring announcement into an exciting story that people actually want to read.
