Brand & Visual Identity Designer

Creative Pulse Studio • Molyko, Buea

About the Role

We aren't looking for someone to just push buttons in Photoshop all day. We need a storyteller who uses colors and shapes to give local businesses a real voice. You’ll be the creative heart of our studio, taking raw ideas from Silicon Mountain founders and turning them into professional brands that people in Buea will recognize instantly.

What you'll be doing

  • Sitting down with local entrepreneurs to understand their vision and turning those conversations into beautiful sketches and mood boards.
  • Crafting high-quality logos, selecting professional color palettes, and picking typography that makes a startup feel like a premium brand.
  • Creating social media templates and layouts that ensure our clients look like a million bucks every time they post.

Requirements

You should have a portfolio that shows you know how to solve problems with design, not just make things look "pretty." Whether you use Canva, Figma, or Adobe, we care more about your eye for detail and your passion for helping Buea’s small businesses scale. If you're the person always fixing people's flyers for fun, you're exactly who we want.
