Banker (Customer Service & Ops)

Regional Financial Hub • Buea Town

About the Role

Banking is built on one thing: trust. We aren't looking for a "human calculator"; we need someone who can handle transactions with precision while making our local traders, students, and workers feel respected and understood. You will be the face of our branch in Buea Town, helping people manage their hard-earned money with confidence and ease.

What you'll be doing

  • Greeting customers and helping them navigate everything from simple deposits to complex digital banking tools.
  • Handling daily transactions with a sharp eye for detail, making sure every Franc is accounted for and every record is clean.
  • Listening to customer needs and explaining our financial services in a way that is clear, honest, and helpful for their personal growth.

Requirements

High integrity is non-negotiable—we need someone who is honest to a fault. You should have a love for numbers and a personality that naturally puts people at ease. Whether you’re a recent graduate in finance or someone with a strong background in customer service, if you’re a problem-solver who double-checks everything, we want to talk to you.
