Civil Engineering Associate

South West Infrastructure Group • Great Soppo, Buea

About the Role

Help us build the actual future of Buea. We aren't just looking for someone to look at blueprints; we need someone who wants to see our roads, drainages, and buildings work better for the community. You’ll be working on projects that people in the South West will use every single day. If you’re ready to get your boots dirty and see your hard work turn into real structures, this is for you.

What you'll be doing

  • Getting out in the field for site inspections to make sure our local construction projects are safe and following the plan.
  • Reviewing technical drawings and assisting our senior engineers with data collection that keeps our projects on track.
  • Liaising with contractors and site workers to ensure the quality of work meets the high standards our city deserves.

Requirements

You should have a degree in Civil Engineering and a sharp eye for spotting technical errors before they become expensive problems. More than the degree, we need someone who is disciplined, reliable, and genuinely cares about the safety and growth of our local infrastructure. If you're a problem-solver who enjoys being on-site as much as being in the office, let’s talk.
