Hotel & Hospitality Manager

Mountain Hotel Suites • Upper Farms, Buea

About the Role

We don’t just want a "boss" in a suit; we want a host who truly cares about people. You will be the face of our guest experience at the foot of the mountain. Whether it’s a tourist visiting for the climb or a business traveler in town for a conference, you’re the person who makes sure their stay feels like a home away from home. If you take pride in making people feel welcome, we’d love to have you lead our team.

What you'll be doing

  • Coordinating with our kitchen and cleaning teams to make sure every room is perfect and every meal is served with a smile.
  • Handling guest check-ins and solving those little "hiccups" that happen during travel so that our guests never have to worry.
  • Ensuring the famous "Buea vibe"—warm, friendly, and professional—is felt in every corner of the hotel.

Requirements

You are a natural "people person" who stays calm and cheerful even when things get busy. Previous experience in hospitality is a plus, but what we value most is your genuine desire to serve others and your ability to lead a team by example. If you’re organized, reliable, and love meeting new people, this is the perfect spot for you.
