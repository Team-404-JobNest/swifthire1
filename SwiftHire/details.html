Associate Frontend Developer

SkyHigh Tech Solutions • Bonduma, Buea

About the Role

Stop building just for your laptop and start building real products used by people right here in Cameroon. We aren't looking for a "coding robot"—we want a fresh thinker who is obsessed with making web pages look as good as they function. We value your hunger to learn over how many years you've spent behind a desk.

What you'll be doing

  • Turning Figma designs into clean, responsive websites that look great on any mobile phone in Molyko.
  • Sitting in on strategy meetings with founders to understand the "why" behind the code.
  • Learning the ropes of a professional dev team, from GitHub workflows to shipping live updates.

Requirements

You should have a solid grasp of HTML5 and CSS3. You don't need to be a master of everything yet, but you should be the type of person who doesn't stop searching until you solve a bug. If you have a GitHub or a live project we can look at, we want to see it!
