Office Operations Assistant

Buea Logistics Hub • Mile 17, Buea

About the Role

Are you the organized friend who always has a plan? We need you to be the "glue" that holds our busy logistics hub together. This isn't just about filing papers; it’s about making sure our team has what they need to succeed and our clients feel taken care of from the moment they walk in. If you take pride in things running smoothly and want to see how a fast-paced business works from the inside, this is the perfect place to start.

What you'll be doing

  • Managing the daily flow of the office, from coordinating deliveries at Mile 17 to keeping our files and records sharp and easy to find.
  • Assisting with basic administrative tasks that keep our hub organized—you'll be the person who knows where everything is and how it works.
  • Being the first point of contact for visitors and partners, ensuring they get a warm, professional welcome to SwiftHire's logistics network.

Requirements

Reliability is your middle name. If you say you’ll handle something, we know it’s done. You should have a basic handle on computer tools (like Word and Email) and a positive attitude that doesn't fade when the office gets busy. If you’re a natural problem-solver who enjoys helping others and keeping things in order, we’d love to have you on board.
